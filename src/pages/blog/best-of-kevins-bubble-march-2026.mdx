## Books

### [Azure Bonds - Kate Novak and Jeff Grubb](https://forgottenrealms.fandom.com/wiki/Azure_Bonds)

A few weeks ago I was fast enough to grab a copy of the forgotten realms books humble bundle. It contained 40 books from 10 series and spreading from 1988 to 2012. This was the first one I read (I thought starting from the earliest is maybe smartest) and oh boy was I positively surprised. I expected nothing and got a strong female main character, a good story and the world building, that of course leverages the forgotten realms lore that existed back then, was just awesome. Looking forward to reading the rest of the books.
