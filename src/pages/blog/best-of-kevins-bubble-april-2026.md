# Best of Kevin's bubble - April 2026

## Links

### [The Last quiet thing](https://www.terrygodier.com/the-last-quiet-thing)

This quite resonated with me. The comparison of "owning" a device with having a relationship with it is also what kept me so long from owning my own bicycle. This is also why I stopped using Instagram & Facebook and went more towards newsletters and RSS feeds. But there I looked in the wrong direction, I specifically like how they say it's not our fault, these products are designed that way. They don't provide yet another way out of the attention madness, but at being aware of it and knowing it's not my fault still feels helpful.

### [Wind Waker in Three.js](https://wind-waker-threejs.com/)

Someone rebuild parts of Zelda: Wind Waker in Three.js so you can play it in the browser. Oh boy did I immediately get caught up and reminded of the time when me and my brother played it on our GameCube. The developer is unfortunately writing on his [GitHub page](https://github.com/Robpayot/tslda) that he is using AI tools to do it, which I guess is a nice way of saying it's vibe coded 😕 But that doesn't make it less fun to play around with 😄

### [Outer Wilds Planet viewer](https://outer-wilds-planetary.vercel.app/)

A colleague of mine sent me this as an old colleague of his made this. If you don't know Outer Wilds, then you really missed out on something. Go to you favorite game store right now, buy it and be gone for the weekend. For everyone that did play this little masterpiece. Click on the link and enjoy the nostalgia while looking at the planets and listening to their songs.
