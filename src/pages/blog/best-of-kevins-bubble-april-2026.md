# Best of Kevin's bubble - April 2026

## Links

### [The Last quiet thing](https://www.terrygodier.com/the-last-quiet-thing)

This quite resonated with me. The comparison of "owning" a device with having a relationship with it is also what kept me so long from owning my own bicycle. This is also why I stopped using Instagram & Facebook and went more towards newsletters and RSS feeds. But there I looked in the wrong direction, I specifically like how they say it's not our fault, these products are designed that way. They don't provide yet another way out of the attention madness, but at being aware of it and knowing it's not my fault still feels helpful.

### [Wind Waker in Three.js](https://wind-waker-threejs.com/)

Someone rebuild parts of Zelda: Wind Waker in Three.js so you can play it in the browser. Oh boy did I immediatly get caught up and reminded of the time when me and my brother played it on our GameCube.
